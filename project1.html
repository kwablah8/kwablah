CONTROL THEORY - Simulating basic electrical systems

This project aims to show how to simulate a basic electrical system. In particular, a black box method based on recorded data and a first principles approach based on the fundamental physics of the circuit will be used.

Both the black box approahch and the accuracy based on the derived models are demonstrated through the use of the related experiment.

Additionally, this activity gives a concrete illustration of the typical class of the first-order systems.
