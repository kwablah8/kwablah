CAD - 3D Hinge Mechanism Design in Fusion 360

This CAD project focuses on creating a 3D hinge mechanism using Autodesk Fusion 360, a powerful 3D modeling and design software. The project aims to design a versatile and functional hinge mechanism that can be used in various applications, such as doors, cabinets, or mechanical assemblies.


Key Project Objectives:

1. Design a robust and aesthetically pleasing hinge mechanism.
2. Ensure precise and smooth movement of the hinge components.
3. Optimize the hinge for strength, durability, and ease of assembly.
4. Utilize Fusion 360's parametric design capabilities to easily customize the hinge for different applications.
5. Create detailed 3D models and assembly animations to visualize the hinge's operation.


Through this project, I will showcase Fusion 360's capabilities in designing and simulating mechanical components, from concept to a fully functional 3D model. The final design will be a valuable resource for engineers, designers, and DIY enthusiasts looking to integrate a reliable hinge mechanism into their projects.
